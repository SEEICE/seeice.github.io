Ice2L2 is a senior design project conducted at Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University under the guidance of Dr.Siebold and Dr.Perrell. It aims to simulate the launching of lunar ice from the surface of the moon to a catching unit orbiting the L2 point for the Simulation Exploration Experience.

A railgun like launcher (not simulated above) will launch a capsule of lunar ice onto a ballistic trajectory, at the end of which it will be caught by a station orbiting the L2 point. This is being simulated using HLA practices, Java, MATLAB, and GMAT. Models done in Blender.
